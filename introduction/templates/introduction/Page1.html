Data Usage
We are kindly requesting you to participate in a financial choice experiment. During the experiment we will ask you to complete a very short demographic survey (your gender and educational major). The experiment is anonymous - we do not collect your name or any other data that can reveal your identity. We also keep the data on a password protected computer and destroy the data within three years from now. You may earn real money in the experiment but you are not exposed to risk of losing money from your own pocket. The experiment does not play a role in the grading of this course and you are free to refuse to participate if you have objections.

Before proceeding to the experiment please complete the data privacy consent form below.
