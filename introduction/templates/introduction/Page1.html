Data Usage
We are kindly requesting you to participate in a financial choice experiment. During the experiment we use your personal data (name, student ID and e-mail) in order to be able to process your bonus points and a potential financial payoff (see below). However, we will anonymize your identity after those awards are handled and not share it with any other party. We also keep the data on a password protected computer and destroy the data within three years from now. You may earn real money in the experiment (as a gift card in Akademibokhandeln) but, of course, you are not exposed to any risk of losing money from your own pocket. The experiment does not play a role in the grading of this course, other than the two bonus points in the MCQ's you will earn if you complete the three tasks.
